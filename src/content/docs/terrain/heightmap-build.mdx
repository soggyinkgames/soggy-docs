### Create a terrain

1. go to `Window/Terrain/Terain Toolbox` in unity
2. scroll to the `Import Heightmap` dropdown and select it.
3. get yourself a heightmap - go to `https://heightmap.skydark.pl/` add the location you want a heightmap of, via name or latitude and longitude 
4. adjust settings to get desired result and note the max height in m and the map size in km.
5. on the website press the `Download PNG Heightmap` icon and rename with preface `Heightmap_[LocationName]_[TerrainWidth]_[MaxHeight]` 
eg `Heightmap_MtFuji_60_3755`

    <Card title="heightmap of Uluru" icon="star">

6. back in unity, create a Terrain folder with another folder inside named after the location of heightmap eg. `Terrain/Geirangerfjord` and place the png in it
7. in the terrain toolbox under `Select Texture` add the heightmap
8. adjust total terrain width and length settings to match your heightmap as well as terrain height, and `Heightmap Resolution` to match image quality under `Group Settings`
9. push `Create`
